Photos

It's always important to enjoy the moments in the present, but it's never a bad idea to take a photo. Capture the moments, and share them with others or reminisce with your friends about the adventures you had together!

I used to have a nikon d3500 for taking photos when travelling, but these days I just use my smartphone camera for convenience.

Hiking

A nice pair of hiking boots will go a long way if you are looking to go hiking often, especially in some areas that get really steep and rocky. The best suggestion I can give it to get a decent sized water bottle to bring with you. Some trips can be long and exhausting, and this is why it is very important that you always STAY HYDRATED!

If you want to get more convenient, you can grab a hydration backpack to carry your supplies, and keep a good amount of fresh water for easy reyhydrating.
