Space Exploration

"To boldly go where no one has gone before."

The Final Frontier

When I was young, I was very interested in space movies and TV series like Star Trek. So much so that my dream job was to become an astronaut and explore space! I had a great interest for learning more about it, and was always excited to watch videos and study it in Physics classes. I think the infinite possibilities is what excites me the most when I think about space exploration. Expanding and exploring the vast unknown. Although I doubt it will entirely solved in my lifetime, I do hope and believe that one day we will be able to travel across our solar system, just like taking a flight across the world.
